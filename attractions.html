Petronas Twin Towers

The Petronas Twin Towers are the most iconic landmark in Kuala Lumpur. They represent Malaysia’s modern development and attract visitors from all over the world.

Batu Caves

Batu Caves is a famous Hindu religious site known for its limestone caves, colourful stairs and large golden statue of Lord Murugan.

KL Tower

KL Tower is a communication tower and tourist attraction that offers panoramic views of Kuala Lumpur’s skyline.
